About

UnConventional Networking was created to give everyday professionals a avenue to help young adults with navigating their lives while getting started with their careers. We also are commited to Dropping In on at risk students with the intent of keeping them from Dropping Out!

We invite you to sign up for yourself or some one you may know who can benefit from our resources. We also would like to grow our Network so we always are looking for more professionals to help us push our mission. We also have tax deductable donations for the professionals who want to help but really aren't sure if they have the time to commit.

Tutoring & Mentorships

We offer 24hr on-line educational tutoring/mentoring for high schoolers, college students and young adults who need assistance navigating through their individual situation. We also provide intense one on one services for students/young adults whom are higher risk upon request and approval. We found that mentored students are 55% less likely to skip school, 78% more likely to volunteer in their communities, and 130% more likely to hold leadership positions.

life coaching

We provide Life Coaching to all of or participants to aide them with all the thing that arise or may arise inside or outside of the educational setting. We understand that alot of our student are dealing with obstacles within there lives where a little coaching and care can make a world of a difference, 80% of coaching clients say that they improved their self-esteem or self-confidence thanks to coaching..

career guidance

Surveys show 93 per cent of the students were aware of less than ten career options to choose from. In contrast, there are more than 600 career options available today. Therefore., the awareness levels of all the available career options for a student need to be increased. We have a large network of professionals from all career paths who are dedicated in seeing our student thrive. These professional will provide our student with the tools and connections needed to set them on a fast track to their career of choice. We also offer financial accountablity classes because we want our students not only to have great careers but also live great lives.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to build a spectrum of young diverse leaders within our global community. We want to provide them with the necessary tools for advancement to lead the world into the next century.
